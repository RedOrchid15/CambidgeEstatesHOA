Welcome to the Cambridge Estates Homeowners Association website.

This website was created to provide Cambridge Estates lot owners/residents with timely information about the community, electronic access to important legal documents, links to other useful sites/information, important contact information and to your Cambridge Estates Homeowners Association Officers/Board of Directors. There is also a link for Lot Improvement requests.

Once again welcome to our website and if you are new to the community, welcome to Cambridge Estates. Let us know if there is additional content you would like to see on the website and how we might improve it. To stay current on happenings and website changes use the RSS feature to link to the site from your computer.

All lot owners are encouraged to obtain access to the website member area.  Please email insert email address here and be sure to include your name, phone number and street address in your request (if you are a non-resident lot owner, please include both your physical address and the address of your Pacific Springs property).  We will send your login credentials back via email.

Sincerely,

The Board of Directors

Cambridge Estates Homeowner’s Association
